<p><b>Replace this page before launch.</b> This boilerplate only includes placeholder legal text and should not be treated as legal advice.</p>

<h2>How to use this placeholder</h2>

<p>Draft a privacy policy that matches your hosting setup, analytics tools, forms, newsletter platform, and any third-party services you use. If this site collects personal data, get the final wording reviewed by someone qualified to advise on your legal obligations.</p>

<h3>At minimum, document:</h3>

<ul class="alt-">
<li>What data you collect</li>
<li>Why you collect it</li>
<li>Where it is stored</li>
<li>Which third parties process it</li>
<li>How people can request access, correction, or deletion</li>
</ul>

<h3>Common places to review</h3>

<p>Check your contact form handling, email delivery, analytics, embedded media, fonts, cookies, newsletter tools, and CMS authentication flow. Those integrations usually decide what your policy needs to say.</p>
