---
question: "Is the investment secured against specific properties?"
answer: >
  Investors participate in the company via a preference share structure. This provides
  priority income rights over ordinary shareholders but does not constitute a direct legal
  charge over individual properties. The strength of the investment is based on the scale
  and valuation of the underlying portfolio, established rental contracts, insurance
  protections where applicable, and conservative capital raising relative to asset value.
