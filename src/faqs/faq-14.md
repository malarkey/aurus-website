question: "Is there a formal investor or shareholder agreement?"
answer: >
  Yes. Investors are bound by legal documents including Aurus Impact Capital’s memorandum
  and articles of association, a subscription agreement, and a hybrid share sale and
  shareholders’ agreement governing the relationship between Aurus Impact Capital,
  Carnegie, and Genivs. These documents define investor rights, return mechanics,
  redemption provisions, and governance.
