---
question: "Is Aurus Impact Capital FCA regulated?"
answer: >
  Aurus Impact Capital is not directly authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA).
  However, the company operates with support from an FCA-regulated administrator for
  oversight, compliance processes, and governance standards. This is a structured
  investment and is not covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS).
